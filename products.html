Aroma-Tabs

The Essence of Flavor.

Aroma-Tabs are a new frontier in culinary exploration. Each tab is a fast-dissolving strip made from edible film, infused with micro-encapsulated aromatic oils. As the tab dissolves on your tongue, it releases a precisely engineered sequence of scents directly to your olfactory system. Since smell accounts for up to 80% of what we perceive as flavor, this allows you to experience the complex essence of foods like 'Himalayan Truffle' or 'Madagascar Vanilla' without any of the calories. It's a tool for chefs, dieters, and the culinarily curious.
